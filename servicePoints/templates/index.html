About ServicePoints

ServicePoints is meant to connect students who are in service-based, student organizations with service opportunities so that they can adjust and continue to effectively serve their communities. Making service opportunities easily accessible gives those who are able to make a difference in their communities the means to do so, so that we can all help each other through this situation.
